Classic Gold Filled Stacking Ring:

These rings are $13 each. Choose the number you want from the "quantity" pull-down.

Elegant, rustic, and original. Each ring is hammered by hand, adding texture and a glittering surface that makes every ring perfect and unique.

Each ring is made with high quality American Made 14k yellow gold filled wire. The gold is bonded to the wire and is over 100 times thicker than high quality plated gold, so these rings are durable & tarnish resistant - suitable for daily wear. Gold filled rings are a beautiful, affordable alternative to solid gold, and can be worn by people with sensitive skin.

- Heirloom quality hammered band of yellow gold.
- I generally recommend these rings in odd numbers: 1,3,5, etc.
- Made to order, just for you.
- Mailed in a cute package suitable for gifting.
